Cyber Security Analyst

Job Description

The Global Cybersecurity team provides enterprise-wide, risk-based security and continuity capabilities to meet changing internal and external threat landscapes. We are responsible for identifying and protecting sensitive information, detecting and responding to cyber threats, and maintaining compliance with regulatory requirements and industry standards. Some job duties and past projects have included: • Creating requirements and design documents • Performing discovery/data analysis and gap analysis • Threat modeling and analyzing solutions to identify security gaps and capabilities • Instrumenting tools to measure, compile and discover security-relevant data • Implementing Proof of Concepts that realize security architectures and solutions • Assisting subject matter experts in researching and developing security assessments of existing solutions as well as defining security architectures for new solutions • Presenting results in front of a wide audience including senior leaders in Visa
